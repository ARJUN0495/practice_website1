Image Classification:

Bounding boxes and segmentation are the two most common methods of annotating Images. These details can then be used for AI’s addressing any kind of market including advertising.

Cuboids

Mainly used in the automotive industry for object detection, which has many applications including collision aversion.

Polygons

This is used for surfaces or objects that don’t have a fixed shape and the data needs to be very precise. In which case bounding boxes cannot be used.

2D & 3D segmentation

This is also used largely in the automotive industry to teach vehicles safe areas to drive and which surfaces to avoid.
